When should I use sun cream to protect yourself from the sunburn ? This interactive map gives you a good answer.  Find the major cities on the map and observe the color, yellow and orange colors indicate that you need a protection. Red and purple colors indicate that you need extra protection. The larger size of the bubble means higher risk of sun burn. This cool black-white base map is from Stamen Design, one of my favorite data visualization design studio based in San Francisco.

 

Tools: Leaflet, Open Street Map, HTML, CSS, Javascript

Challenges: Dynamic symbol Change, Data conversion
