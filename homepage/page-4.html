Leopold Area Map
→

This interactive map shows the area of Leopold, where the Aldo Leopold Foundation is located. This detailed area map is one of the product from Bordner Project, it demonstrates the historical agricultural and forest cover including the types of tree species, density and tree diameters. It has multiple layers showing cultural and physical features, providing a pretty detailed understanding of local landscape.

 

Tools: ArcMap, ArcGIS portal, ArcGIS online

Challenges:  Data extraction, online configuration
